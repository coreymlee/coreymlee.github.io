Corey Lee is a designer based in San Francisco. As a partner at cLabs, he works on Valora and Celo. His work is based on understanding users and the models they bring to their interactions—intuitive equals familiar.

He’s worked on some of the world’s most beloved products like Acrobat and PDF and has been an early employee at some successful and less successful startups. In a much too brief period in NYC, he helped launch Feedie, which would go on to fund over 12M school millions. It was, for the time, a pretty novel way to productize social media.
