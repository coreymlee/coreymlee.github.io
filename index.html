About

I do product research and design with Adobe. Our team develops innovation projects for the Document Cloud. Mostly, we spend time talking with users and trying to understand the models they bring to their interactions.

From 2012 to 2015, I ran a small consultancy designing and building things with Brief, Salesforce, the de Young Museum, and others. Before that, I did research with Moment and designed interfaces for Location Labs.

In the fall of 2013, I was in-residence at the Tokyo Innovators House, Impact Japan's incubator space.

I also helped launch Feedie, an awesome campaign that's now funded over 10 million school meals.

Selected work

  • Brief
    Worked early with founders, Jared and Martin, to define a new way to organize files. It used machine learning to index all of a person's files, wherever they're stored, and autogenerate human-centered metadata.
  • Salesforce
    Distilled user research findings into functional insights for the redesign of Salesforce.com. From the learnings, we developed a new user-oriented taxonomy and global navigation. We helped merge their new visual design system with a fresh modular UX library.
