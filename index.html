Selected work

  • Brief
    Worked with the early startup's founders, Jared and Martin, to research and design Brief. It used machine learning to index all of a person's files, wherever they're stored, and autogenerate human-centered metadata.
  • Salesforce
    Distilled user research findings into functional insights for the redesign of Salesforce.com. From the learnings, we developed a new user-oriented taxonomy and global navigation. We helped merge their new visual design system with a fresh modular UX library.
  • Morgan Stanley

  • de Young Museum
    Collaborated with the museum's Brinker Ferguson on a few projects, including an in-gallery piece for the Thomas G. Fowler collection. The interface allows visitors to flip through the late collector's sketchbook and "touch" artifacts found in the exhibit.
  • The Lunchbox Fund
    Feedie transforms passion for sharing food photos into sharing real food for those in need. The campaign, led by Mario Batali and Jamie Oliver, supports the Lunchbox Fund, a non-profit that encourages school attendance in South Africa by providing daily lunches for vulnerable children.

Personal Projects

  • Habits and Disruptions (MFA Thesis)
    My thesis studied habit disruptions as a means to understand behavior and research the logic behind people's interactions.
  • Impact Skyline
    Recipient of a $10K Social Entrepreneurship Grant from the Center for Art and Public Life, Impact Skyline was an ethnographic research and service design project with Skyline High School in Oakland, CA.
  • Impact Foundation Tokyo
    I spent the fall of 2013 in-residence at Impact House, an incubator space in the Omotesando neighborhood of Tokyo. During my stay, I created Topo. The Foundation runs TEDxTokyo and Global Entrepreneurship Week.
  • Lub Dub
    A pillow that pulses with a partner's heartbeat.
  • Elevator and 66edorp were attempts to see if changing a device's screen interface could change its ergonomics and evolve a "new" device.
  • N1 Stripe
    A theme hack for the Nylas N1 email client that rearranges UI layout, tidies up visuals, and adds a few subtle animations. (2 whole stars on GitHub! )
  • The Other End
    An IRL game using fiducial IDs to transform a corridor into an active race track.
  • Topo
    A plugin to view Wikipedia articles as topographic maps of importance.
  • Vannevar
    A browser plugin to aid Wikipedia reading by making it a little more linear.
  • Skim
    A plugin offering a few ways to visually trim NYT articles and maybe improve reading efficiency.
  • Morning Data
    A prototype for a data-infused mirror.
  • Kiosk
