Corey Lee is a designer based in San Francisco. He's a partner at cLabs, where he works on Valora and Celo. His work is based on understanding users and the models they bring to their interactions. He believes intuitive equals familiar.

He’s contributed to some of the world’s most beloved products including Acrobat and PDF and has been an early employee at a few successful startups and failed adventures. During a short stint in NYC, he helped launch Feedie, which would go on to fund over 12M school meals.
